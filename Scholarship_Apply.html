The scholarship, known as the Bright Future Scholarship, is awarded annually to students who demonstrate exceptional academic achievements and a strong commitment to community service. Founded in 2010 by a group of local educators, this scholarship aims to support students pursuing higher education in various fields such as science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Recipients of the scholarship not only receive financial assistance towards their tuition fees but also benefit from mentorship opportunities and career guidance.
